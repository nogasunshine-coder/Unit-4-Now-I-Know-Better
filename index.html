Now I Know Better – Summary
Alice was 17. Her life was perfect. She was a good student and popular with her friends. She sang, danced and played the guitar.

On April 10, 2018, Mark came to pick her up. Mark smelled of alcohol, but she still got into his car. That was her first mistake.

Mark was driving very fast. He lost control of the car and it overturned. Mark and Brenda wore seat belts. Alice didn't. That was her second mistake.

Alice was badly hurt. She woke up in the hospital and couldn't feel her legs. Two weeks later, doctors said: "She is out of danger, but she will never walk again."

Mark went to prison. Alice was in a wheelchair forever because of him – or so she thought.

Then Brenda said: "You're wrong." And Alice understood – it was her fault, too. She used to think that nothing bad could ever happen to her. Now she knows better.
